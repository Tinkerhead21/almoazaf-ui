Feature Outline

We need a dashboard that allows managers to see the real-time availability of their team members. This should integrate with the existing leave management system to show who is currently on vacation, sick leave, or out of office.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Dashboard displays a list of all team members.
  • Status indicators show 'Available', 'On Leave', 'Busy' (synced with calendar).
  • Managers can filter by sub-teams or specific dates.
  • Integration with Azure DevOps for tracking associated tasks.
